Nigerian Fried Rice And Chicken

Chicken fried rice is the comfort dish of Nigeria food. This classic take on the favorite is easy to make and makes the perfect lunch or dinner. Don't want chicken? Make it with or go beef or goat! .

Totally time to prepare

PREPEARATION OF THE DISH

  1. In a medium skillet over medium heat, heat olive oil.
  2. Season chicken with salt and pepper on both sides
  3. Then add to skillet, and cook until golden and no longer pink, 8 minutes per side
  4. Remove from skillet and let rest 5 minutes, then cut into bite-sized pieces.
  5. To the same skillet, heat 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  6. Add onion and carrots and cook until soft, 5 minutes
  7. Add garlic and ginger and cook until fragrant, 1 minute more.
  8. Stir in rice and peas and cook until warmed through, 2 minutes.
  9. Push rice to one side of skillet and add remaining tablespoon sesame oil to the other side
  10. Add chicken back to skillet with soy sauce and green onions and stir to combine.
