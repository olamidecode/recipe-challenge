Nigerian Fried Rice And Chicken

Chicken fried rice is the comfort dish of Nigeria food. This classic take on the favorite is easy to make and makes the perfect lunch or dinner. Don't want chicken? Make it with or go beef or goat! .

Totally time to prepare

YIELDS

SERVINGS PREP TIME TOTAL TIME
6 servings 0 hours 40 mins 1 HOURS 20 MINS

INGREDIENTS

  • Any part of the chicken that you feel like using
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Vegetable oil,
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 2 boiled corn
  • Tyhme
  • 4 c. cooked white rice (preferably leftover)
  • 1 packect offrozen peas
  • Curry
  • Diced liver
  • 2 green peppers
  • Know cubes
  • Rice
  • boiled chicken gravy

PREPEARATION OF THE DISH

  1. In a pot size of your choice add your oil and let it heat up.
  2. Season chicken with salt,maggi,tyhme and curry on both sides
  3. Then boil the chicken in a serparate pot for 25mins(depending on your chicken quantity)
  4. when the oil in the pot has heated up add your diced up oinions,green peppers and allow them to fry for 10mins
  5. when the oinions and peppers have fried for 10 mins add your rice into it and allow that to cook for 10min.
  6. After the add the boiled chicken gravy into the rice and cook it for 10 mins
  7. Add about 3 cups of water to the rice and your diced carrots,diced liver and your corn.
  8. Add curry,tyhme,know and salt to the rice
  9. Stir the rice then cover it with the pot cover
  11. Add chicken back to skillet with soy sauce and green onions and stir to combine.
